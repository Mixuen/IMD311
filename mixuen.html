How Mixuen came to be
Maisarah Yixuen loved drawing. In fact, it runs in the family. Growing up she explored various interests wherever she could find them, taking influence from the likes of anime and novels and mixing different genres into her artistic works. Most of her artwork were heavily inspired by pop art, science fiction as well as fantasy. Whenever she is not drawing in her free time, Maisarah enjoys going out with friends and family. The outside world is where she was able to take loads of inspiration in.

Mixuen is a portmanteau of Maisarah's first two names. It was originally a gametag of a playable character in an MMORPG. Maisarah started to use the name to replace an older pseudonym for her works, and is now working towards creating Mixuen a brand of her own.
